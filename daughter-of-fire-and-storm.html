A Feud Amongst Gods.
A war between the gods sealed Themesis in a tomb beneath Kraagswell Mountain, bound by the life and breath of Magic Wielders. But Suppressors hunt them, and the bonds are crumbling.

The Chosen One.
Avoiding the Suppressors that roamed Howling Cove was easy for Lillyanna. Until the night she’s called out for being a Magic Wielder. Fleeing for her life she runs into a man as mysterious as he is alluring. He promises her safety, but how safe can she feel when he knows her name and everything about her?

Her Protector.
Damiyun is Lillyanna’s Keeper, a task he never wanted. Sworn to protect her, he must bring her to Kraagswell Mountain so she can fulfill her destiny. A simple task. Except it’s not. Between the danger of Suppressors, and his rising feelings, he receives far more trouble than the bargain was worth.

A World In Peril.
The road is long and dangerous, and the bonds grow weaker. Demons and Suppressors hunt them. Danger lurks at every corner. The fate of the world rests on Lillyanna and Damiyun’s shoulders. Time is running out. For everyone.
