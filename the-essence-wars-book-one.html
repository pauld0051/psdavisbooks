P. S. Davis

Author of The Essence Wars

Release December 2025

Maerwyn was meant to lead a small command on the sea. Instead, she's sent to a southern fort, a punishment wrapped in orders. Her task is simple: hold a fractured outpost until reinforcements arrive.

But the battle has already come. When she reaches the fort, it’s fallen. The siege is over. The gates are broken. What remains inside is not an enemy force, but something worse. Something unseen. And there are no survivors.

Forced into uneasy alliance with two enemy soldiers and their bonded beasts, Maerwyn must uncover what really happened at the fort before it happens again. But the deeper they search, the clearer it becomes: the true enemy isn’t who they were sent to fight.

A story of loyalty, survival, and the shifting cost of war in a world where unity may be the deadliest ambition of all.
