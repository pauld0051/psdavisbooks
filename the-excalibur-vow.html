Forged in myth. Lost in time.

Blacksmith Wystan sharpens Excalibur—and breaks off a sliver. Before he can set things right, Arthur falls at Camlann. With the king gone, Wystan vows to return the sliver to the next ruler or the Lady of the Lake. But magic intervenes, hurling him into modern-day England.

The country is divided over whether to dissolve the monarchy. If Wystan can’t recover the sword or find the true heir, the magic will disappear—and he’ll be stranded in a time that’s not his own.

While Wystan searches for Excalibur and a way home, a secret knight society and a power-hungry witch scheme to seize the throne—and the sword—for themselves.

The Excalibur Vow debuts late spring, early summer. Magic, myth, and monarchy collide in this modern YA Arthurian fantasy.
