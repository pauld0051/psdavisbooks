P. S. Davis

Author of The Seeker’s Wrath

Release June 30th

Captain Marcius Saylong has a vision: to unite the North with the fractured Western Order before it tears itself apart.

But peace comes at a cost. Secret pacts with pirates spiral into bloodshed. Castles fall. Heirs vanish. And as the Order’s foundation cracks, Saylong must outpace betrayal, uncover a conspiracy to unseat the realm’s leader—and stand trial for the unity he dares to forge.

The North will rise or the West will burn.

An epic of loyalty, blood, and the price of unity in a world built for war.
